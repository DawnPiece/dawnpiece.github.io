# Armor

Custom armor drops from NPCs throughout the story. Each set has 4 pieces: Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots.

## Armor Stats

Damage reduction follows this formula:

> **Reduction** = Armor ÷ (Armor + 100)  
> **Damage Taken** = Incoming Damage × (1 − Reduction)

There are no gear classes and armor is based on tier only. You can mix and match pieces from different tiers.

| Tier      | Armor Per Set | Armor Per Piece |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------|
| Common    | 8             | 2               |
| Uncommon  | 16            | 4               |
| Rare      | 32            | 8               |
| Epic      | 64            | 16              |
| Legendary | 128           | 32              |
| Mythical  | 256           | 64              |

## Armor Upgrading

Requires Belly, Damage Received, and the Required Orb. Mythical tier cannot be upgraded.

| Tier      | Damage Received | Orb Needed | Belly Cost  |
|-----------|-----------------|------------|-------------|
| Common    | 1,000           | Uncommon   | 2,000       |
| Uncommon  | 5,000           | Rare       | 10,000      |
| Rare      | 25,000          | Epic       | 50,000      |
| Epic      | 125,000         | Legendary  | 250,000     |
| Legendary | 500,000         | Mythical   | 1,250,000   |

## Armor Repairing

Durability decreases by one per hit taken. At zero durability, armor provides no protection. Repair your armor anytime at a Forge which costs 1 Belly per 10 durability (rounded up) or 1 Belly per 20 durability at a Mythical Forge.

| Tier      | Durability | Cost at Zero   | Mythical Forge Cost at Zero |
|-----------|------------|----------------|-----------------------------|
| Common    | 200        | 20 Belly       | 10 Belly        |
| Uncommon  | 400        | 40 Belly       | 20 Belly        |
| Rare      | 800        | 80 Belly       | 40 Belly        |
| Epic      | 1,600      | 160 Belly      | 80 Belly        |
| Legendary | 3,200      | 320 Belly      | 160 Belly       |
| Mythical  | 6,400      | 640 Belly      | 320 Belly       |

## Gear Set Bonuses

Wearing all 4 pieces of the same character set activates a Set Bonus. When mixing tiers, the bonus uses the lowest tier's value. Weapons are not part of set bonuses. See the [Set Bonus Page](/Gear/SetBonus) for the full list.

## Gear Tier Bonuses

Knockback Resistance is based on your lowest equipped tier (no set matching needed).

- Epic+: 20% Knockback Resistance
- Legendary+: 40% Knockback Resistance
- Mythical+: 60% Knockback Resistance
