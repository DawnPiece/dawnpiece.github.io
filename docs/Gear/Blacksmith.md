# Blacksmiths

Blacksmiths are Essential NPCs found at various islands. To see which islands have one, check the Essential NPCs section in the [Island Guide](/Islands/Overview).

Every Forge has 5 functions: Upgrade, Repair, Salvage, Roll, and Construct. The Mythical Forge found at the [Black Market](/Islands/BlackMarket) adds a 6th function for Runes.

## Upgrading

To upgrade gear, you need Belly, Damage Dealt/Received, and the Required Orb. The interface shows exact requirements when you place an item inside.

- Regular Forge: upgrades up to Legendary Tier
- Mythical Forge: upgrades up to Mythical Tier

See detailed requirements:
- [Weapon Upgrade Requirements](/Gear/MeleeWeapons.html#weapon-upgrading)
- [Armor Upgrade Requirements](/Gear/Armor.html#armor-upgrading)

## Repairing

Costs 1 Belly per 10 durability (rounds up). Gear never breaks so at zero durability it simply stops working (weapons deal 0 damage, armor provides 0 protection).

- Regular Forge: 1 Belly for every 10 durability
- Mythical Forge: 1 Belly for every 20 durability (half cost)

See detailed durability tables:
- [Weapon Repairing](/Gear/MeleeWeapons.html#weapon-repairing)
- [Armor Repairing](/Gear/Armor.html#armor-repairing)

## Salvaging

Payout is based on Tier, Remaining Durability, and Damage Dealt/Received. Each durability point below max subtracts 1 Belly. Each 100 Damage Dealt/Received adds 1 Belly.

| Gear Tier | Forge Payout | Mythical Forge Payout (×1.2) |
|-----------|--------------|------------------------------|
| Common    | 100 Belly    | 120 Belly                    |
| Uncommon  | 500 Belly    | 600 Belly                    |
| Rare      | 2,000 Belly  | 2,500 Belly                  |
| Epic      | 10,000 Belly | 12,000 Belly                 |
| Legendary | 50,000 Belly | 60,000 Belly                 |
| Mythical  | 250,000 Belly| 300,000 Belly                |

## Rolling

Insert an orb to roll for a random gear piece of the matching tier.

- Regular Forge: Common Orbs only
- Mythical Forge: Any orb tier for gear of that tier

## Constructing

Combine shards into orbs of the same tier, or orbs into a higher tier orb.

| Orb       | Forge Shards | Forge Orbs | Mythical Forge Shards | Mythical Forge Orbs   |
|-----------|--------------|------------|-----------------------|-----------------------|
| Common    | 10 Common    | —          | 8 Common              | —                     |
| Uncommon  | 10 Uncommon  | 5 Common   | 8 Uncommon            | 4 Common              |
| Rare      | 10 Rare      | 5 Uncommon | 8 Rare                | 4 Uncommon            |
| Epic      | 10 Epic      | 5 Rare     | 8 Epic                | 4 Rare                |
| Legendary | 10 Legendary | 5 Epic     | 8 Legendary           | 4 Epic                |
| Mythical  | 10 Mythical  | 5 Legendary| 8 Mythical            | 4 Legendary           |

## Runes

Runes are Mythical-Tier set bonuses that can be purchased on the [Black Market](/Islands/BlackMarket) and applied to Mythical armor pieces. Each rune adds an extra set bonus, for up to 5 total bonuses (1 original + 4 runes on each individual armor piece). See the [Set Bonus Page](/Gear/SetBonus) for available bonuses. The restrictions for runes are below:

1. Mythical Set Required: Must have at least 3 Mythical armor pieces equipped to your character to apply a rune.
2. No Duplicate Bonus: Can't apply a rune matching the armor's base set bonus.
3. No Duplicate Runes: Can't equip two armor pieces with the same rune bonus.
4. Unique Set: All 4 rune bonuses and the original set bonus must be different.
