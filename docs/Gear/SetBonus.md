# Set Bonuses

Set Bonuses activate when wearing all 4 pieces of the same character's gear set. The bonus value displays in the item tooltip. When mixing tiers, the lowest tier's value applies. Some gear sets have no bonus yet. These are marked as "None" and reserved for future bonuses.

## Guard Sets

Reduce incoming damage from specific sources. This percentage is applied after armor damage reduction.

| Set Bonus         | Reduces               | Common | Uncommon | Rare | Epic | Legendary | Mythical |

| Elemental Guard   | Elemental Damage      | 1%     | 2%       | 4%   | 8%   | 16%       | 32%      |
| Melee Guard       | Melee Damage          | 1%     | 2%       | 4%   | 8%   | 16%       | 32%      |
| Projectile Guard  | Projectile Damage     | 1%     | 2%       | 4%   | 8%   | 16%       | 32%      |
| Bludgeon Guard    | Bludgeon Weapon Damage| 2%     | 4%       | 8%   | 16%  | 32%       | 64%      |
| Float Guard       | Float Weapon Damage   | 2%     | 4%       | 8%   | 16%  | 32%       | 64%      |
| Impact Guard      | Impact Weapon Damage  | 2%     | 4%       | 8%   | 16%  | 32%       | 64%      |
| Pierce Guard      | Pierce Weapon Damage  | 2%     | 4%       | 8%   | 16%  | 32%       | 64%      |
| Pull Guard        | Pull Weapon Damage    | 2%     | 4%       | 8%   | 16%  | 32%       | 64%      |
| Slash Guard       | Slash Weapon Damage   | 2%     | 4%       | 8%   | 16%  | 32%       | 64%      |
