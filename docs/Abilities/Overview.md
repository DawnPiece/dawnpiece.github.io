# Abilities Guide

The DawnPiece mod features a full ability system covering Haki, Fighting Styles, Races and Devil Fruits, plus General Stats tied to your character. Each system has its own leveling structure.

Use the links below to explore each section:

- 📊 [General Stats](Stats.md) — Health, Stamina, and Hunger
- 🧬 [Races](Race.md) — Multiple Races with 6 tiers from Common to Mythical
- ⚔️ [Fighting Styles](Style.md) — Multiple Styles that reach up to level 60
- 🌀 [Haki](Haki.md) — Observation, Armament, and Conqueror's Haki Information
- 🍈 [Devil Fruits](DevilFruit.md) — Multiple Devil Fruits with 6 tiers from Common to Mythical
