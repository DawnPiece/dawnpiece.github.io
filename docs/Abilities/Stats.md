# General Stats

Three stats govern your character Health, Stamina, and Hunger. Each levels up independently through different actions.

## Health

Start at 20 HP and has a max of 500 HP. Levels up by taking damage in general. You gain 1 HP per 500 damage received.

| Level | HP   | Damage Taken This Level | Cumulative |
|-------|------|-------------------------|------------|
| 0     | 20   | 0                       | 0          |
| 1     | 50   | 15,000                  | 15,000     |
| 2     | 100  | 25,000                  | 40,000     |
| 3–9   | +50  | 25,000 each             | —          |
| 10    | 500  | 25,000                  | 240,000    |

## Stamina

Start at 50 and has a max of 150. Levels up by traveling. You gain 1 stamina and +1% speed per 5,000m traveled. Max level doubles all speeds (movement, swim, fly, sprint). Each melee attack costs 5 stamina. At zero stamina, you get 50% slowness and can't use melee weapons.

| Level | Stamina | Speed | Distance This Level | Cumulative |
|-------|---------|-------|---------------------|------------|
| 0     | 50      | 0%    | 0                   | 0          |
| 1     | 60      | 10%   | 50,000              | 50,000     |
| 2     | 70      | 20%   | 50,000              | 100,000    |
| 3–9   | +10     | +10%  | 50,000 each         | —          |
| 10    | 150     | 100%  | 50,000              | 500,000    |

## Hunger

Start at 20 and has a max of 100. Levels up by eating. You gain 1 hunger per 500 hunger points replenished. Hunger works like vanilla but also fuels Haki abilities. Using Haki costs hunger. At zero hunger, you get nausea.

| Level | Hunger | Replenished This Level | Cumulative |
|-------|--------|------------------------|------------|
| 0     | 20     | 0                      | 0          |
| 1     | 25     | 2,500                  | 2,500      |
| 2     | 30     | 2,500                  | 5,000      |
| 3     | 35     | 2,500                  | 7,500      |
| 4     | 40     | 2,500                  | 10,000     |
| 5     | 50     | 5,000                  | 15,000     |
| 6–9   | +10    | 5,000 each             | —          |
| 10    | 100    | 5,000                  | 40,000     |
