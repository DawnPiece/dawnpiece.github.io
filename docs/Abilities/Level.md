# Character Level

Your Character Level is the sum of all experience gained across Health, Stamina, Hunger, Haki, Races, Fighting Styles, and Devil Fruits.

> **Total XP(Level) = Level³**

Max level is 1000, requiring 1,000,000,000 total XP. Early levels come quickly, the real grind starts past 500.
