# Haki

Three types of Haki: Observation, Armament, and Conqueror's.

## Leveling

- Observation: Levels up by taking damage
- Armament: Levels up by dealing damage
- Conqueror's: Unlocks when Observation and Armament reach level 10. Levels by dealing damage with any Haki ability.

Ability unlocks: Observation and Armament gain 1 ability every 2 levels for 5 total. Conqueror's starts with 1 ability, then 1 every 2 levels for 6 total. 

## Skill Points

Each level up unlocks skill points you can apply to boost damage, protection, range and reduces cooldowns for that specific ability.
