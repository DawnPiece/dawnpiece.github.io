# Devil Fruits

Devil Fruits provide the player with a plethora of abilities. They aren't automatically unlocked. Each fruit has its own leveling and skill tree with a max level of 10.

## Getting a Devil Fruit

| Method | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Buy    | Purchase Crate Keys from Devil Fruit Merchants with Belly  |
| Earn   | Farm fragments and Construct a Devil Fruit from them       |
| Trade  | Trade with other players via Discord or the in-game market |
| Win    | [Random Server Events](https://discord.com/channels/1385526041807949855/1388360081640521850) |

## Leveling

Each fruit levels independently so you can switch fruits and always retain your experience and skill levels for all fruits you already leveled up. Higher-tier fruits require more XP to level up. Unlocking all abilities requires reaching max fruit level.

All devil fruits level up by using the abilities. Experience is tracked by the damage dealt, damage protected, health restored, distance covered from dashes, etc. 

## Skill Points

Skill points can be assigned in the Main Menu. Each ability maxes out at Level 5 costing 10 skill points total. Maxing out all abilities requires reaching max fruit level.

## Awakenings

When a player is level 10, their devil fruit is considered awakened. To help reach this goal, there are awakening shards that can be farmed from high level bosses or gained from completing achievements. Each awakening shard can be eaten to add 50,000 XP to your currently active devil fruit.
