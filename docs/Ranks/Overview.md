# Life Paths

Choose a life path to follow by opening the main menu and selecting your path. Your in-game suffix and chat color will reflect your choice. As you gain bounty from quests and events, your life path ranks up automatically which will increase your weekly rewards.

## Weekly Rewards

| Bounty Required | Weekly Rewards                                    |
|-----------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| Starting        | 1,000 Belly, 1 Common Orb, 4 Sea King Meat        |
| 50,000,000      | 2,500 Belly, 1 Uncommon Orb, 8 Sea King Meat      |
| 200,000,000     | 10,000 Belly, 1 Rare Orb, 16 Sea King Meat        |
| 500,000,000     | 25,000 Belly, 1 Epic Orb, 32 Sea King Meat        |
| 1,000,000,000   | 50,000 Belly, 1 Legendary Orb, 64 Sea King Meat   |
| 2,000,000,000   | 100,000 Belly, 2 Legendary Orbs, 96 Sea King Meat |
| 4,000,000,000   | 200,000 Belly, 3 Legendary Orbs, 128 Sea King Meat|
