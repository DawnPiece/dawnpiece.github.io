# General Level

There is a General Level that is associated with your overall character, that is separate but integrated with the level of each ability. The General Level is essentially the sum of all levels. Please see the information below based on the abilities in game.

### Max Level Calculation

| Ability Type  | Ability Trees | Max Level per Tree | Cumulative Max Level |
|---------------|---------------|--------------------|----------------------|
| General Stats | 3             | 10                 | 30                   |
| Race          | 6             | 10                 | 60                   |
| Fighting Style| 1             | 60                 | 60                   |
| Haki          | 3             | 10                 | 30                   |
| Devil Fruits  | 0             | 10                 | 0                    |

The Current Max Level that your character can be is Level 180.
