# Race

Players will be able to choose a race that they will like to play as in game. To begin with, players will have access to choose a common starter race, and maxing out a race races will allow you to unlock more races of the next tier to use in the future. Unlockable races will be stronger at their base value.

### Race Leveling

Race leveling is tied to the amount of experience points by the player when using any race move. Experience points are tied to damage dealt for damage moves, damage recieved for armor moves, health regeneration for heal moves, distance traveled for speed moves, and so on and so forth.<br>

Each race will have its own level save data, so if a player changes their race, they start the new race at level 0. If they change back to a race they used before, then they return to that race’s level save data. A mythical race will require more experience points to level up compared to a common race. The max level is 10 for each race.<br>

### Skill Points

You can access the DawnPiece Main Menu in game by clicking the M keybind by default. From there click the Skill Tree option and you can see all of the skills available to upgrade your current active race. Each skill will start at Level 1 and have the potential to be upgraded to Level 5. Each upgrade costs more skill points than the last, and each ability will require a total of 10 Skill Points to max each one out. Reaching the max level of the race will see that you unlock all skill points for that specific race.

Every ability level up will increase damage, protection, range or other positive stats depending on the move. For example, a damage move will start with it's original damage based on its level, and when it reaches level 5 it will do 100% of the damage. Every level up will also decrease cooldown, debuffs, and other negative stats depending on the move. For example, a move may have a cooldown of 15 seconds and when its level 5 it drops to 5 seconds.
