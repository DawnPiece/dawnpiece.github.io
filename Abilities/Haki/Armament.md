# Armament Haki

Armament Haki is a rare spiritual ability that allows the user to coat their body or weapons in an invisible armor known as Busoshoku. Through training, users develop the ability to harden their body parts and weapons into a black, steel-like coating, significantly enhancing their offensive and defensive capabilities. This page lists all abilities associated with Armament Haki.

### Requirements

Armament Haki is automatically unlocked but must be leveled up before any of the abilities below can be used. Some abilities may have specific requirements or situations they can be used in listed below.

### Level 2: Coating

Coating is unlocked at Armament Level 2. The user coats their fist in a black hardened coating, significantly increasing the damage dealt by unarmed strikes for the duration of the ability. This ability requires the right hand to be empty in order to be activated.

| 4     | +16             | 16 Seconds | 34 Seconds | 14 Points   |
| 5     | +20             | 20 Seconds | 30 Seconds | 10 Points   |

### Level 4: Imbuing

Imbuing is unlocked at Armament Level 4. The user channels Armament Haki into a held sword, coating the blade in a black hardened layer that greatly increases the damage dealt by sword strikes for the duration of the ability. This ability requires a sword item to be held in order to be activated.

### Level 6: Emission

Emission is unlocked at Armament Level 6. The user projects their Armament Haki outward from a body part, releasing a powerful invisible burst of force in the immediate vicinity. Any entity caught within the blast radius is knocked back with the force of a very powerful punch. This ability requires the right hand to be empty in order to be activated.

| 4     | 5 Blocks     | Medium-High     | 34 Seconds | 34 Points   |
| 5     | 6 Blocks     | High            | 30 Seconds | 30 Points   |

### Level 8: Internal Destruction

Internal Destruction is unlocked at Armament Level 8. The user focuses their Armament Haki inward on impact, bypassing the target's resistances and dealing a massive amount of damage with their next punch. This ability requires the right hand to be empty in order to be activated.

### Level 10: Full Body Hardening

Full Body Hardening is unlocked at Armament Level 10. The user extends their Armament Haki across their entire body, encasing themselves in a full black hardened shell that tremendously increases defense against all incoming damage for the duration. Full Body Coating cannot be cancelled once activated.
