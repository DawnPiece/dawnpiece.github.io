# Observation Haki

Observation Haki is a rare spiritual ability that allows the user to sense the presence, intentions, and actions of others. Through training, users develop heightened awareness, allowing them to detect enemies through obstacles, anticipate attacks before they happen, and eventually glimpse brief moments into the future. This page lists all abilities associated with Observation Haki.

### Requirements

Observation Haki is automatically unlocked but must be leveled up before any of the abilities below can be used. Some abilities may have specific requirements or situations they can be used in listed below.

### Level 2: Mantra

Mantra is unlocked at Observation Level 2. The user expands their awareness outward, sensing all living entities within range regardless of walls or obstacles. Detected entities are highlighted for the duration of the ability. Invisible entities can also be detected.

### Level 10: Observation Domain

Observation Domain is unlocked at Observation Level 10. The user creates a field of heightened awareness centered on themselves. While active, all Observation abilities are enhanced. Nearby entities are continuously detected, invisible targets are revealed, incoming attacks are predicted, and Future Sight remains active for the duration. Observation Domain cannot be cancelled once activated.
