# Conqueror's Haki

Conqueror's Haki is an extremely rare spiritual ability said to be possessed by only one in several million people. Users can project their willpower outward, overwhelming the minds and bodies of those around them. This page lists all abilities associated with Conqueror's Haki.

### Requirements

Conqueror's Haki is unlocked upon reaching Level 10 in both Observation Haki and Armament Haki. Some abilities may have specific requirements or situations they can be used in listed below.

### Level 0: Observation Killing

Observation Killing is unlocked immediately upon obtaining Conqueror's Haki. The user exerts their willpower outward to actively suppress their own presence, rendering themselves completely undetectable by any Observation Haki ability for the duration. Observation Haki users in the vicinity will find their abilities unable to perceive the user for as long as Observation Killing remains active.

| 2     | 11 Seconds | 39 Seconds | 49 Points   |

| 4     | 16 Seconds | 34 Seconds | 44 Points   |
| 5     | 20 Seconds | 30 Seconds | 40 Points   |

### Level 4: Incapacitation

Supreme King Clash is unlocked at Conqueror's Level 10. For the duration, all attacks deal greatly amplified damage while the user is encased in a supreme Conqueror's coating. Supreme King Clash cannot be cancelled once activated.
