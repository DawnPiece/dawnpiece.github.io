# Devil Fruits

Players will have access to use Devil Fruits in the game. Unlike races and classes, devil fruits are not automatically accessible and each player will have to get their hands on one.

### Getting A Devil Fruit

There are four main ways to get a Devil Fruit.

1. Buying Devil Fruits: You can use in-game currency that you get from killing enemies and completing quests to buy Crate Keys from the merchants at Goa Kingdom, which you can then use to roll for a random fruit depending on it's tier. 
2. Earning Devil Fruits: Some bosses have a low chance of dropping fragments of their devil fruits. You can farm these bosses and trade their fragments in for the entire fruit to the fragment traders at Goa Kingdom.
3. Trading Devil Fruits: Trading with or buying fruits from other players in the Market Channels on Discord or in game is a great way to get a devil fruit. You can also sell your possessions to other players to save up for a fruit or even a crate key.
4. Winning Devil Fruits: There may be occasional events and giveaways where you can win a devil fruit! We don't do these often and they come at random times so keep an eye out on the [Events Channel](https://discord.com/channels/1385526041807949855/1388360081640521850) in the Discord.

### Devil Fruit Leveling

Devil Fruit leveling is tied to the amount of experience points by the player when using any Devil Fruit move. Experience points are tied to damage dealt for damage moves, damage recieved for armor moves, health regeneration for heal moves, distance traveled for speed moves, and so on and so forth.<br>

Each Devil Fruit will have its own level save data, so if a player changes their Devil Fruit, they start the new Devil Fruit at level 0. If they change back to a Devil Fruit they used before, then they return to that Devil Fruit’s level save data. A mythical Devil Fruit will require more experience points to level up compared to a common Devil Fruit. The max level is 10 for each Devil Fruit.<br>

### Skill Points

You can access the DawnPiece Main Menu in game by clicking the M keybind by default. From there click the Skill Tree option and you can see all of the skills available to upgrade your current active Devil Fruit. Each skill will start at Level 1 and have the potential to be upgraded to Level 5. Each upgrade costs more skill points than the last, and each ability will require a total of 10 Skill Points to max each one out. Reaching the max level of the Devil Fruit will see that you unlock all skill points for that specific Devil Fruit.

Every ability level up will increase damage, protection, range or other positive stats depending on the move. For example, a damage move will start with it's original damage based on its level, and when it reaches level 5 it will do 100% of the damage. Every level up will also decrease cooldown, debuffs, and other negative stats depending on the move. For example, a move may have a cooldown of 15 seconds and when its level 5 it drops to 5 seconds.
