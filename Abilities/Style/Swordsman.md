# Swordsman Style

The Swordsman Style is based on abilities that the Akazaya, Hatchan, Hyouzou, Rockstar, Cavendish, Hyogoro, Kozuki Oden and Roronoa Zoro use. This page lists all of the abilities associated with the Swordsman Style.

### Passives

All Slash Type Weapons deal an extra 1 damage per hit per level. Upon reaching the max style level 60, Slash Weapons will do an additional 60 damage per melee hit.

### Requirements

This style is automatically unlocked. You are required to be holding a Slash Type Weapon to use all Swordsman abilities. Some abilities may have specific requirements that are listed below.

### Level 0: Modoku no Ken

Modoku no Ken is the first automatically unlocked ability for the Swordsman Style. The ability is passive and activates in the background whenever the player deals a melee hit with a Slash Type Weapon. Each successful melee hit applies a stacking poison DoT to the target that ticks once per second. The poison duration resets on each new hit. The poison cannot stack with itself meaning hitting again refreshes the timer rather than adding a new stack.
