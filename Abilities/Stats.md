# General Stats

There are three types of General Stats in the game: Health, Stamina and Hunger.

### Health

1. Players start with 20 health points and can achieve a maximum of 500 health points. Health is leveled based on the amount of damage received by the player.
2. While levels are cut and dry for general leveling purposes, health will increase in the background by 1 HP for every 500 Damage Received.

### Stamina

1. Every level up of stamina increases movement speed, swim speed, fly speed, sprint speed and sprint distance. Max level doubles all speeds compared to level zero.
2. While levels are cut and dry for leveling purposes, speed will increase in the background by 1% for every 5,000M traveled.

### Hunger

1. Players start with 20 hunger points and can achieve a maximum of 100 hunger points. Hunger is leveled based on the amount of hunger points replenished by the player.
2. Hunger works the same way as it would in vanilla, however it will now be related to Haki as well. Using Haki Abilities costs hunger, and reching zero hunger will make you have nausea. Hunger is replenished by eating food.
3. While levels are cut and dry for general leveling purposes, hunger will increase in the background by 1 hunger point for every 500 Hunger Points Replenished.
