# Lunarian Race

The Lunarian Race is a mythical tier race based on the legendary Lunarians who once ruled atop the Red Line. Lunarians possess unmatched control over divine flames, incredible durability, powerful regenerative abilities, and complete aerial supremacy. This page lists all of the abilities associated with the Lunarian Race.

### Requirements

This race is unlocked once you reach the max level for at least one legendary tier race. Some abilities may have specific requirements or situations they can be used in listed below.

### Level 0: Eternal Flame

Eternal Flame is the first automatically unlocked ability for the Lunarian Race. The player channels their eternal flame, entering one of two combat states that can be toggled at will. When Eternal Flame is active, the player gains increased damage reduction and immunity to all fire damage and burning effects, but loses the movement speed bonus normally granted by their lineage. When Eternal Flame is inactive, the player gains increased movement speed but does not receive damage reduction or fire immunity. The ability can be manually toggled between these states at any time.

| Level | Damage Reduction | Speed Bonus |
|-------|------------------|-------------|
| 1     | 30%              | 20%         |
| 2     | 33%              | 22%         |
| 3     | 37%              | 25%         |
| 4     | 42%              | 28%         |
| 5     | 50%              | 30%         |

### Level 0: Imperial Flame Burst

Imperial Flame Burst is the second automatically unlocked ability for the Lunarian Race. The player releases a massive explosion of divine flame centered on their current position. All enemies within the blast radius take direct damage and are set on fire for 5 seconds. The fire damage does not stack and instead refreshes the duration if reapplied.

|-------|--------|--------------|---------------------|------------|
| 1     | 60     | 6 Blocks     | 5                   | 40 Seconds |
| 2     | 66     | 7 Blocks     | 6                   | 39 Seconds |
| 3     | 78     | 8 Blocks     | 7                   | 37 Seconds |
| 4     | 96     | 10 Blocks    | 8                   | 34 Seconds |
| 5     | 120    | 12 Blocks    | 10                  | 30 Seconds |

### Level 2: Sky Dominion

Sky Dominion is unlocked at Lunarian Level 2. The player fully embraces their Lunarian heritage and gains true flight while the ability is active. Flight speed is greatly increased while Sky Dominion is active. Sky Dominion cannot be active at the same time as Eternal Flame. The ability can be toggled on and off manually.

| Level | Flight Speed Bonus |
|-------|--------------------|
| 1     | 40%                |
| 2     | 41%                |
| 3     | 43%                |
| 4     | 46%                |
| 5     | 50%                |

### Level 4: Blazing Dive

Blazing Dive is unlocked at Lunarian Level 4. The player launches downward at extreme speed while engulfed in divine flames. Upon impact, a fiery shockwave erupts from the point of contact, damaging all nearby enemies. The player is immune to fall damage during the descent.

Divine Regeneration is unlocked at Lunarian Level 6. While Eternal Flame is active, the player can channel their Lunarian vitality to rapidly heal over time. Divine Regeneration cannot be activated while Eternal Flame is inactive.

### Level 8: Flame Guard

Flame Guard is unlocked at Lunarian Level 8. The player creates a shield of divine flame around themselves. The shield absorbs incoming damage until its health is depleted or the duration expires. While the shield is active, a portion of absorbed damage is converted into healing for the player.

### Level 10: Divine Cataclysm

Divine Cataclysm is unlocked at Lunarian Level 10. The player calls forth a devastating firestorm from the heavens. After charging, waves of divine flames rain down across a large area, damaging all enemies struck by the firestorm. Enemies hit are also set on fire for 5 seconds. The charge cannot be cancelled once started.
