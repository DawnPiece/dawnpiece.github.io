# Human Race

The Human Race is a common tier race that is based on the Rokushiki abilities Cipher Pol uses. This page lists all of the abilities associated with the Human Race.

### Requirements

This style is automatically unlocked. Some abilities may have specific requirements or situations they can be used in listed below.

### Level 0: Soru

Soru is the first automatically unlocked ability for the Human Race. The ability allows the player to dash in the direction they're aiming at.

| Level | Cooldown   | Distance   |
|-------|------------|------------|
| 1     | 30 Seconds | 10 Blocks  |
| 2     | 29 Seconds | 11 Blocks  |
| 3     | 27 Seconds | 13 Blocks  |
| 4     | 24 Seconds | 16 Blocks  |
| 5     | 20 Seconds | 20 Blocks  |

### Level 0: Tekkai

Tekkai is the second automatically unlocked ability for the Human Race. The ability allows the player to go into defense mode taking less damage but cannot use any other abilities while active and is significantly slowed down.

| Level | Damage Protection | Speed Reduction | Time Active | Cooldown   |

| 3     | 30%               | 70%             | 13 Seconds  | 57 Seconds |
| 4     | 35%               | 65%             | 16 Seconds  | 54 Seconds |
| 5     | 40%               | 60%             | 20 Seconds  | 50 Seconds |
